Cherry

A single cherry slab with its full live edge preserved on one side — the natural bark-line silhouette running the length of the piece. Book-matched to double the figure, it's kept deliberately simple: slender hairpin legs let the wood carry the room. Commissioned for an entryway in a mid-century home in Wash Park.
